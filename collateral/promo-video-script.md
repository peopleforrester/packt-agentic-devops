## Shot notes

- Vertical 9:16. Frame head and shoulders, eyes to lens.
- Even, soft front light. Avoid backlight and window glare.
- Quiet room. Phone on a tripod or propped steady.
- One clean take is enough; energy over polish.
- Keep it under sixty seconds. If you run long, cut the second sentence.
